Creamy chicken pasta with chicken

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Chicken
  • Heavy cream
  • 2 Onions
  • Black pepper
  • Shredded cheese of your choice(As much as you'd like)

Steps

  1. Put water to boil, add oil and salt to the water and after it boils cook the pasta as said on the pasta box
  2. Add a little bit of oil in a pan on a high heat, add cut onions and brown them
  3. Put in cubed chicken to the onion and add salt and pepper
  4. Stir the chicken on all sides and when ready add heavy cream
  5. When the cream starts bubbling add small amounts of shredded cheese and mix
  6. After your sauce with chicken is ready, put the pasta in and mix it
  7. Enjoy!
