Fried chops

Ingredients

  • Pork chops
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Salt your porkchops and put your oil on medium-high heat
  2. Take the porkchops and coat them all in your flour of choice (Anything can get the job done really)
  3. Beat some eggs in a bowl (one egg for 3 porkchops) and coat them in it
  4. Coat the porkchops in breadcrumbs
  5. Put oil in a pan and put in on medium-high heat and wait until hot
  6. Put the porkchops in the oil, wait until you see that the side is golden and flip it
  7. Wait until you see that the chop is fully golden, take it out and put a paper towel on it to drench the oil
  8. Enjoy!
