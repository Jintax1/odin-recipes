How to make rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Put 2 medium cups of rice in a strainer and clean with water
  2. Put a small amount of oil in a pot with high heat with cut onions and stir every 30 seconds until brown
  3. Put the rice in and stir it for 30 seconds
  4. Add 3 cups of water in the pot, add salt and wait until the water starts to boil, turn the heat off put the lid on and wait
  5. DON'T STIR THE RICE!
  6. After 10-15 minutes the rice should be done
  7. Enjoy!
