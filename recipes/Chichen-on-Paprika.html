Chicken on paprika with rice

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Heavy cream
  • Onions
  • Oil
  • Black pepper and Sweet paprika

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Add a little bit of oil in a pan on a high heat, add cut onions and brown them
  3. Put in cubed chicken to the onion and add salt, pepper and paprika
  4. Stir the chicken on all sides and when ready add heavy cream
  5. Now that you have both, put them in a bowl or a plate
  6. Enjoy!
